Your State Privacy Rights

State consumer privacy laws may provide their residents with additional rights regarding our use of their personal information.

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (“CCPA”) and California's “Shine the Light” law (Civil Code Section § 1798.83), California residents have the following rights:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected, used, shared or sold: You have the right to request a list of the categories of your personal information we have collected about you. You are also entitled to receive a) information identifying all third-party companies to whom EWP may have disclosed, within the last 12 months, personal information pertaining to you and your family; and b) a description of the categories of personal information disclosed. You can also find a list of the categories of information we collect and disclose about you elsewhere in this Policy. To request a list of the information we collect about you, send an email to support@ewproject.org. Once the request has been received, you may be asked to provide additional personal information for verification purposes before your request can be accommodated. Requests to access your personal information may be submitted up to two times in a rolling twelve-month period.
  • Right to request deletion of personal information: You have the right to request that we delete any personal information we have collected about you provided we do not need to retain the information for legitimate business or legal reasons and direct our service providers to do the same. To request that your information be deleted from our system, please send an email with your request to support@ewproject.org . Once the request has been received, you may be asked to provide additional identifying information for verification purposes before your request can be accommodated.
  • Right to opt-out of the sale or sharing of personal information: EWP does not sell information that directly identifies you. However, the CCPA’s broad definitions of “sale” and “personal information” may deem the common flow of information regarding digital analytics and advertising platforms to be a sale. You can request to opt-out of the sale of your personal information to an outside party by visiting here. For purposes of this Privacy Policy, “sell” means the sale, rental, release, disclosure, dissemination, availability, transfer, or other oral, written, or electronic communication of your personal information to an outside party for monetary or other valuable consideration, subject to certain exceptions in applicable law. We may also use cookies and related technology which could constitute sharing or a sale of your personal information.
  • Right to correct personal information: You have the right to ask that EWP correct the personal information we may have collected about you if that information is incorrect. EWP reserves the right to request identifying information from you in certain circumstances, as permitted by law.
  • Right to non-discrimination in terms of price or service when a consumer exercises a privacy right under CCPA: EWP respects and values the privacy rights of our consumers, and we do not discriminate against customers who exercise their privacy rights under California law or any other law.
  • Authorized Agent: As a California consumer, you have the right to have someone else make requests for you under the CCPA. If you would like to use an authorized agent to exercise your rights for you, we may require documentation demonstrating your agent’s authority to submit requests on your behalf.
