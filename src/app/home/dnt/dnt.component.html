Do Not Track

Extraordinary Women Project does not track our website's users during use, accross third party websites or otherwise to provide targeted advertisement. For this reason Extraordinary Women Project also does not respond to Do Not Track or DNT signals. However, some third party applications may track your browsing activities to tailor advertisements presented to you. If you would wish, you can change your browser settings to set your DNT signal to not allow for advertisers to track your usage. To learn more about DNT settings, Extraordinary Women Project recommends visiting allaboutdnt.com for more information
